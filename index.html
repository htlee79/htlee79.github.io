Hyungtae Lee

I am a research scientist and federal employee at DEVCOM Army Research Laboratory.

I received my PhD from University of Maryland, where I was supervised by Prof. Larry S. Davis. I received my MS from KAIST under the supervision of HyunWook Park and my BS from Sogang University.

Email  /  CV  /  Google Scholar  /  Github

Research Interests

I'm interested in the fields of computer vision and machine learning, including but not limited to the development of recognition approaches in various tasks such as 2D/3D object detection, segmentation, human pose estimation, hyperspectral image classification, etc. Currently, I am very interested in studying how to leverage virtual images in model training based on domain adaptation, transfer learning, and generative AI, etc.
